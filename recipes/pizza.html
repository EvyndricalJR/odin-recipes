Buffalo Style Chicken Pizza

Description

This Buffalo chicken pizza is a delicious combination of two favorite foods: Buffalo chicken wings and pizza!

Ingredients

  • 3 cooked chicken breasts, diced
  • 1 (2 ounce) bottle hot sauce
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle blue cheese salad dressing
  • 1 (16 inch) prepared pizza crust
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Preparation Steps

  1. Gather the Ingredients
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F
  3. Mix chicken, hot sauce, and melted butter in a bowl unitl well combined
  4. Place crust onto a rimmed baking sheet or pizza pan. Spread salad dressing over crust
  5. Top with chicken and sprinkle with mozzarella
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly, about 5 to 10 minutes. Let site a few minutes before slicing.
