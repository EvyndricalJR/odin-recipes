Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These pumpkin chocolate chip cookies are perfect for those of us who love all things pumpkin pie and chocolate. These cake-like cookies are always a big hit. I think they taste best when they are cold from the refrigerator.

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin
  • Sugar
  • Oil
  • Egg
  • Leaveners
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt
  • Chocolate

Preparation Steps

  1. Combine the wet ingredients. Dissolve the baking soda in milk, then stir it in.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients, then stir the mixture into the wet ingredients.
  3. Stir the walnuts, chocolate and vanilla.
  4. Drop the dough on the cookie sheets and bake until the cookies are firm.
